who we are?

Our company is specialized in software development and distribution, implementation of ICT solutions, digital marketing and online promotion. We are partners for e-commerce solutions and on-line sales.

Our company is specialized in software development and distribution, implementation of ICT solutions, digital marketing and online promotion. We are partners for e-commerce solutions and on-line sales.

We are creating sale strategies, defining market niches and the organization and management of the sales process on the principle of Key Account Management.

In early 2017, our operations have expanded to neighboring Albania, by opening subsidiary in Tirana - Amplitudo sh.p.k, with a similar portfolio and activities. If you have a business idea which includes appearance on Albanian market, please contact us to share our experiences with you.

As a result of our cooperation with BIO ICT Institute of Electrical Engineering in Podgorica, we are expecting commercialization of several innovative projects such as smart buoys, solar panels for charging mobile phones on beach umbrellas, noise sensor, etc.

In addition, through the support of partner companies Orion Telekom (Telecommunications - Internet, IPTV and fixed telephony services) and Lotus project (project management, design) we can provide a wide portfolio of services to our clients, both in Montenegro and in the region.

Information security management system

Quality management system
